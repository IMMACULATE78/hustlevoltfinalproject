How do I price my services competitively in the African market?

I'm a graphic designer based in Kenya and struggling to find the right pricing strategy. How do I balance competitive rates with fair compensation?

Expert Answer by Adaora Okafor

Start by researching global rates for your skill level, then adjust based on your local cost of living. I recommend starting at 60-70% of US rates and gradually increasing as you build your portfolio and testimonials. Focus on value-based pricing rather than hourly rates when possible.

Best payment methods for receiving international payments?

I'm based in Nigeria and want to start freelancing for international clients. What are the most reliable and cost-effective payment methods?

Expert Answer by Kwame Asante

I highly recommend Payoneer for Nigerian freelancers. Low fees, reliable transfers, and you can get a local NGN account. Wise is also excellent for multi-currency needs.
